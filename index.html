Digital Explorers SIG

Welcome to the Duke OLLI Digital Explorers SIG website. This site includes information related to the purpose/scope of this SIG, the SIG's meeting schedule, resource/reference material for upcoming and past meetings.

Please treat all content on this site as an initial/first draft. And a partial/incomplete draft at that! At this stage in "setting up" this new SIG, my focus with the content on this site is planning the initial set of meetings that will be scheduled for the Spring 2026 term.

At this time I am only sharing this site with OLLI members who participated in the Fall 2025 "Tech Skills 101" course and OLLI staff I am working with to establish this new SIG. (OLLI staff is planning to publish announcements about this new SIG to the Duke OLLI community in December 2025; I intend to have this site's contents refined enough to share directly with the OLLI community at large around the same time.)

If you are an OLLI member who participated in the Fall 2025 "Tech Skills 101" course, I have shared the URL to this site with you so that you can act as a reviewer of the draft content and contribute ideas for the SIG meetings we plan/schedule for the Spring 2026 term. At present, there are two specific pages currently on this site I invite you to review and share any feedback you have:

  • The proposed sessions page: Here you will find the ideas I've come up with for meeting topics/themes. I also have some draft language on this page related how we can structure each meeting's time and three possible "flavors" of meetings.
  • The schedule page: On this page, you will find the dates for the SIG meetings we will have and proposed session topics for them.

To access these pages, first click/tap the menu icon () found in the blue bar at the top of this site's pages. Then click "Schedule" or "Sessions" to access the associate page.

If you choose to review this draft content and want to provide feedback including, please do so via email to olli.shamlin@gmail.com.
